# Cognitive Biases to Probe

Checklist. Before concluding a branch, explicitly ask whether any of these are in play.

## Confirmation Bias
- **Probe:** "What evidence would prove this plan wrong, and have you looked for it?"
- **Probe:** "What is the strongest argument against this approach?"

## Sunk Cost
- **Probe:** "If you hadn't already invested in X, would you still choose it today?"
- **Probe:** "Is the past work a reason to continue, or a separate decision?"

## False Dichotomy
- **Probe:** "Is this really an either/or choice, or is there a third option?"
- **Probe:** "What happens if you do neither A nor B?"

## Overconfidence in Estimates
- **Probe:** "Have you doubled the timeline or halved the throughput estimate to stress-test it?"
- **Probe:** "What is the 90th-percentile worst-case outcome?"

## Status Quo Bias (Hidden Technical Debt)
- **Probe:** "Are you preserving this pattern because it is good, or because it is familiar?"
- **Probe:** "If you were designing this from scratch today, what would you change?"

## Groupthink / Authority Bias
- **Probe:** "Is this decision driven by data or by who proposed it?"
- **Probe:** "Who disagrees with this plan, and what do they see that you don't?"
